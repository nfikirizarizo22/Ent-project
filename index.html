Short Description

This project compares two types of bread commonly found in Rwanda: Industry Made Breads (a high-standard product) and local bread from unregulated bakeries (a substandard product). The goal is to identify quality differences and propose improvements in local production.

Bread Comparison Table

Media

Recommendations for Quality Improvement

  • Maintain hygiene in food preparation and packaging
  • Use food-safe gloves and clean tools
  • Train bakery staff on Rwanda FDA food hygiene standards
  • Label products with ingredient list and expiry date
  • Refer to Rwanda Standards Board (RSB) for certification (S-mark)
  • Read Rwanda FDA guidelines: "General Principles of Food Hygiene"
